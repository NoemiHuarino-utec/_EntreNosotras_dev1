# 🌎 The Problem

In Peru, menstruation is still surrounded by stigma and misinformation.

* **99% of girls feel ashamed of their period at school**
* **81% of boys believe menstruation is a disease**
* Thousands of girls miss school every year due to lack of menstrual education.

Stories like *María*, a 12-year-old girl who can lose up to **20% of her school year** simply because no one explained what was happening to her body, inspired us to build a solution.

Silence has a cost — and girls are paying it with their education.

---

# 💡 Our Solution

**EntreNosotras** is designed as a **“Digital Big Sister”** — a safe and private digital space where girls can ask questions, learn about their bodies, and plan their lives with confidence.

The platform focuses on **education, autonomy, and privacy**.

# ✨ Key Features

💬 **AI Assistant (24/7)**
An anonymous AI assistant that answers questions about sexual and reproductive health in a safe and stigma-free way.

📅 **Smart Cycle Prediction**
Helps users understand their menstrual cycle and anticipate hormonal phases to avoid unexpected school absences.

📚 **Autonomy Library**
Educational resources that translate complex medical information into clear, human language.

🔒 **Privacy First**
Designed to ensure that no sensitive questions are linked to a user's real identity.

# 🤖 AI Assistant

The EntreNosotras assistant is powered by the **OpenAI ChatGPT API**.

We implemented a **Large Language Model (LLM)** with a specialized **system prompt** designed to act as a safe educational guide for girls and adolescents seeking information about menstrual and reproductive health.

The prompt was carefully designed to:

* Provide accurate sexual and reproductive health education
* Use simple and friendly language instead of medical jargon
* Maintain a supportive and non-judgmental tone
* Protect user privacy by avoiding personal data collection

The AI acts as a **trusted digital "big sister"**, offering guidance and information while encouraging users to seek professional medical support when needed.

### Example questions the AI can answer

* Why do I get cramps during my period?
* Is it normal for my cycle to be irregular?
* Why does menstrual blood sometimes look brown?
* How long should a period normally last?

# 🏫 Business Model

EntreNosotras follows a **B2B + Public Access model**.

The platform is offered to:

* Schools
* NGOs working with adolescent health
* Municipalities and public education programs

A single institutional contract can provide access to **hundreds of girls at once**.

At the same time, the platform remains **free for individual users**, allowing us to gather real usage insights and expand impact.

# 💻 Run the Project Locally

Clone the repository:

git clone https://github.com/your-username/entre-nosotras.git
cd entre-nosotras

Install dependencies:

npm install

Create a `.env` file in the root folder and add your API key:

OPENAI_API_KEY=your_api_key_here

Run the development server:

npm run dev

The application will be available at:

http://localhost:3000
